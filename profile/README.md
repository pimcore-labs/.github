# Pimcore Labs

**The best stuff you've never seen.**

Prototypes, crazy ideas, experimental integrations, half-finished apps — the things
that usually never leave a laptop. This is where we open them up and share them.

## Two Labs, One Mission

| | |
|---|---|
| [**Pimcore Lab**](https://github.com/orgs/pimcore-labs/repositories) | AI-driven prototypes and experimental integrations built by the Pimcore team. |
| [**Community Lab**](https://github.com/pimcore-labs/.github/blob/main/Community-Lab.md) | Open innovation from partners, developers and the wider community. |

## Not Production Ready — And That's The Point

Everything here is a proof of concept. It exists to show what's possible, to spark
a conversation, and to get real feedback fast — not to run your business.

## The Ground Rules

- **POCL licensed** — public, free, open
- **No product guarantees** — no SLA, no support, no roadmap commitment
- **No auto-graduation** — a lab project becomes a product only through a full, deliberate review
- **Minimal overhead** — fast and iterative, days instead of months

Clear rules > maximum freedom.

## The Innovation Engine

Idea → prototype in days → validate with real customers → feedback → product and
roadmap input. A closed loop: what proves itself out here can find its way into
Pimcore. What doesn't, taught us something anyway.

## Get Involved

Fork it, break it, build on it, show us what you've made. If it makes someone say
*"wait — Pimcore can do THIS?"*, it belongs here.
